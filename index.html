7 Segment Counter

In the Fall of 2016 I embarked on my EE studies and in one of the classes there arose a problem that dealt with digital numbers and how they are manifested.

Our final project for Digital Logics class was to apply the fundamental way human logic is expressed in electronics to make a counter that displays itself by interpreting high/low signals corresponding to our decimal numbers into segments of a digital number display.

It was intense and fascinating - I developed a great teamworking relationship with my partner in crime. At first the specs of the project seemed daunting, but at the very core of an endeavor are simple steps. I have learned that by working together, we can create what was unimaginable at first.

When we completed the project successfully it was very rewarding and reinforced my desire to understand more applications of modern conveniences I took for granted before because I thought it was too complex.
